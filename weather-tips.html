☀️ Weather Safety Tips

Be prepared for any season with expert-approved weather advice!

Heatwaves

Stay hydrated. Wear light-colored clothing and avoid outdoor activities during peak hours.

Heavy Rain

Carry waterproof gear. Avoid driving in flooded areas and unplug electronics during thunderstorms.

Snow & Ice

Layer up. Use salt or sand on pathways and drive cautiously on icy roads.

High Winds

Secure outdoor items. Stay indoors and avoid trees, power lines, and large windows.
